# Question 1

Who is known as the Missile Man of India?

A. C. V. Raman  
B. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam  
C. Homi Bhabha  
D. S. N. Bose

---

# Answer 1

**B. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam**

- aerospace scientist
- major role in Indian missile development
- remembered for science, simplicity, and inspiration

# Question 2

Who won the Nobel Prize in Physics for work on light scattering?

A. C. V. Raman  
B. Vikram Sarabhai  
C. Jagadish Chandra Bose  
D. Srinivasa Ramanujan

---

# Answer 2

**A. C. V. Raman**

- Raman Effect
- major scientific milestone from India
- great example of curiosity and observation
